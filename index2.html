New Bethel CME Youth Outreach Program

2023


Winter Mini-Camp

Summary: Students learned how to assemble and keep their own disk launchers.
Topics covered: Math,Physics,3D-modeling,Engineering,Presentation Skills, Teamwork and FUN!





Fall Trunk-Or-Treat

Summary: Provide students with a safe place to go trick or treating.
Hall of Fame Award winning FIRST Robotics Team 191 showcased their robot to promote STEM and give back to the community.
Topics covered: Community Engagement and FUN!





Summer Camp and Seabreeze

Summary: Students learned how to program microcontrolers, create 3D-models and program in HTML.
A celebration of all of the hard work was a field trip to Seabreeze.
Topics covered: Math,Physics,3D-modeling,Engineering,Presentation Skills, Teamwork and FUN.
